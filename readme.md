## Crypto 201 — Advanced Study Curriculum

This repository is a curated, advanced study program designed to take you from proficient to expert across the most important domains in cryptocurrency. It’s structured as a self-paced, research-grade curriculum that blends technical foundations, market structure, and the practical mechanics that matter in production systems and trading.

### Who this is for
- Practitioners seeking a rigorous, up-to-date reference across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, L1 design, DA/MEV, DeFi, stablecoins, custody, and trading
- Engineers, researchers, and investors who want depth, not hype

### How to use this curriculum
- Read sequentially for a full end-to-end arc, or jump to the part most relevant to your current focus.
- Each part is split into concise chapters; follow cross-references for deeper context.

## Very brief summary
A concise, expert-oriented curriculum covering: Bitcoin’s design and scaling; Ethereum’s PoS, rollups, and account abstraction; Solana’s high-performance architecture; L1 design trade-offs; data availability and MEV; Hyperliquid’s vertically integrated perps stack; core DeFi mechanisms; stablecoins and yield construction; institutional custody; and practical market structure and trading. Read end-to-end to develop a systems-level understanding of how modern crypto actually works—and how to operate effectively within it.
